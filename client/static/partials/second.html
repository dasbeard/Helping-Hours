How to find your Latitude and Longitude:

  1. Open Google maps in another tab or window and find your location.
  2. On the map, right click on the correct location and select 'Whats here?'.
  3. A window should appear at the bottom of the map with the address as well as the Latitude and Longitude (i.e. 34.184450, -118.448286).
